What is SEO ?
	SEO stands for “search engine optimization.” In simple terms, it means the process of improving your site to increase its 	visibility for relevant searches.

What is SILO ?
	A SILO structure is a type of deep site architecture that I find to be very logically organized. The hierarchical groupings are determined by topics and subtopics.

Crawl budget is the number of pages that Google will crawl. This is really important for big sites and sites that need updates often.
